FRAMING SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
General Requirements
  • Hard hats, safety glasses, and steel-toe boots required at all times.
  • Fall protection required at heights of 6 feet or greater.
  • No site-built scaffolding permitted.
  • All wall panels and trusses must be properly braced.
  • Unsafe conditions must be corrected immediately.
Fall Protection Enforcement
  • 1st Offense: $500 per person
  • 2nd Offense: $1,000 + 1 month suspension
  • 3rd Offense: $1,500 + 1 month suspension
  • 4th Offense: $2,000 + 1 year suspension
  • 5th Offense: Removal from BFS approved list

SEVERE WEATHER REQUIREMENTS
High Wind & Storm Protocol
  • Wall panels and trusses must be properly braced at all times.
  • Loose materials must be secured prior to storm events.
  • High wind warnings require immediate jobsite review.
  • If materials fall due to improper bracing, automatic rebuild required.
  • Superintendent must be notified immediately of weather damage.
Stop Work Authority

Work must be paused if unsafe weather conditions develop. No production schedule overrides safety requirements.

COLD WEATHER POLICY
Heating & Fire Safety
  • Propane heaters are strictly prohibited.
  • Burn piles are not permitted on job sites.
  • Open flames are not allowed within framed structures.
  • Temporary heating equipment must comply with OSHA standards.
Worker Protection
  • Proper cold weather PPE must be worn.
  • Hydration must be maintained even in cold conditions.

ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES
B. Competent Person Requirement
